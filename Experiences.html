DECEMBER 2022

On 09 DECEMBER 2022 , I have given an oppurtunity to be a referee for Kejohanan Taekwondo under Persatuan Taekwondo Negeri Kelantan (PTNK) for 2 days. Well, of course to be a referee, you need to take a referee course that will be held twice in one year. Being a referee is not easy because you need to know at least a bit of basic kyorugi and need to know how to handle the player. I am a trainee referee which I'm still learning on how to be better. Being a referee is really exhausting because you have to really see if the player make a mistake or not, which in short, their result of win and lose depending on the referee itself.
